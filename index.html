BRIDAL STUDIO & ACADEMY

Welcome to Vasantham Beauty Care & Academy, Karur's most trusted destination for 100% Organic and Herbal beauty care. Led by the expertise of Mrs. Jayanthi K, we bring you a blend of traditional herbal wisdom and modern bridal artistry. We believe that every woman deserves to glow naturally without the fear of harmful chemicals.


Our studio is not just about makeup; it’s about providing a healing touch to your skin and hair through our signature homemade products. Whether you are a bride-to-be or someone looking for professional beauty training, Vasantham is here to guide your journey to perfection.

Our Commitment to You

Organic First

We use 100% chemical-free, homemade herbal products for all our treatments ensuring zero side effects.

Award Winning

Recognized with the Saadhanayaalar Award for excellence in bridal makeup and service quality.

Expert Training

We are an NTSDI Authorized centre providing government-certified courses to empower future beauticians.

"My mission is to help every woman find her natural beauty while maintaining healthy skin. Our academy aims to build a community of skilled professionals who value quality and safety over shortcuts." — Mrs. Jayanthi K (Founder)
